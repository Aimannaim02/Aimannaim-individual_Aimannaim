My Simple Education Summary

I started my studies at the primary level, starting in a kindergarten called Tadika Kemas. I was there for 2 years in 2007 and 2008.


Next I extended the lesson to an elementary school memorial. I used to study at 2 schools in different states because I moved after joining the Northern Corridor Program. My first school was SK Seri Muda in Sungai Korok, Jitra. I gained knowledge there for 4 years from 2009 to 2012. In 2013, I was in grade 5, I moved to SRK Putera in Kangar, Perlis for 2 years and sat for the UPSR there.


After graduating from primary school, I entered the realm of secondary school where I entered a religious school. I started Form 1 at SMK Agama Arau, Arau, Perlis until I graduated. I sat for the PT3 and SPM exams there.


After got a fairly excellent SPM result, I entered the university world where I took this field which is Library Management (IM120).
