1) Lived in a dormitory for 11 years.

I used to live in a dormitory when I was still in primary school at the age of 11. You must be wondering why I entered a dormitory so young hehe. I was selected to be one of 60 people to be part of the government assistance program which is Program Koridor Utara. I got a lot of help from each other there.

2) Participate in sports.

During form 1, I joined a petanque club at school. At first, I knew nothing about the sport and never knew at all. Until I tried to play with my teacher. I became interested and played regularly. One time, I was just excited to participate in the competition at the state level. Unexpectedly I made it to the semi -finals. Since then, I often participated in it until I got the first place at the Perlis state level after 3 years of training.

3) Experience working in an office.

Last semester, I had to undergo industrial training during the semester break. I chose to undergo industrial training at the library at UUM. There, I could feel the atmosphere of working in the office and gained a lot of experience while trying on every part of the library. I learned many things while undergoing industrial training
